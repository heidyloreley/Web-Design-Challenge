Latitude vs Cloudiness

This is an analysis for the comparison between the latitude and the percentage of cloudiness registered.

There is no general assumption of a relationship between the location of a city (its latitude) and the percentage of cloudiness that could exist.

The graph obtain in this analysis clarifies that there is no visible evidence that there is a relationship between these two variables. We can find low and high levels of cloudiness, from no cloudiness at all up to 100% of clouds, along all the cities analyzed (from latitude -60° to latitude 80°).
