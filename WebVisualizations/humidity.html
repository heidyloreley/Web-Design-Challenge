Latitude vs Humidity

This is an analysis for the comparison between the latitude and the percentage of humidity registered.

Another general assumption is that the humidity is high closer to the equator, because it is warmer in there.

The graph created in the analysis does not support this assumption. We can see that there are cities with 100% of humidity along almost all latitudes, from -50° up to almost 80° of latitude. In this case we may have to consider other variables, rather than latitude, to assume a high humidity, for example, closeness to bodies of water like the ocean.
