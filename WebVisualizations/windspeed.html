Latitude vs Wind Speed

This is an analysis for the comparison between the latitude and the speed of wind registered.

There is also no general assumption of a relationship between the location of a city (its latitude) and its wind speed, measured in miles per hour (mph).

This analysis confirms that there is no evident relationship between these two variables. The closeness to the equator, or distance, does not relate with the wind speed a city could experience.
